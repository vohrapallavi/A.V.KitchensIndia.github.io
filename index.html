Overview

For years A.V Kitchens India has been serving the Hospitality industry with pride and perfection. We undertake projects from planning of kitchens, to manufacturing and installation of food service equipment.Our team of engineers makes sure that each of our equipment is a pleasure to use for every chef. We also undertake customized fabrication of kitchen equipment. The company uses top 16 SWG stainless steel 304 under the highest quality standard of manufacturing process. We represent leading international foodservice equipment companies.
Our complete range of products include cooking ranges, bulk cooking systems, combi ovens and steamers, exhaust systems, refrigeration units, bain moire, plate dispensers, dish washing machines, baking ovens, mixers, milk and water boilers, sandwich grillers, coffee machine etc.
Our range of Indian & imported foodservice equipment provide one stop solution for all kitchen requirements. Our quick response after sale service is up to the highest level of satisfaction.
